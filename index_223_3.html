⚠️ Before You Import
⚠️ Automatic import is not perfect
The PDF parser does its best to extract data from your legacy estimates, but due to differences in PDF formatting it may not always capture everything correctly.

After importing you must carefully check:
  • All labour lines — types, hours and rates
  • All parts — descriptions, part numbers and prices
  • Vehicle details — reg, make, model and VIN
  • Customer name and contact details
  • The total matches the original estimate
The imported estimate will be saved as a Draft and you will be offered the chance to review and edit it immediately after import.
